DIA KINETS

Project-reflection about the role of Soviet diafilms before and after the beginning of the full-scale invasion in Ukraine

PROJECT NAME

What is Dia Kinets

“Dia” — the word comes from the Greek
language “through.”

“Kinets” — the transliteration of the
word, with which each diafilm (filmstrip)
ends. It means “the end.”

Diafilm — a series of dispositive (slide
films), combined by a certain theme
in one filmstrip that consists
of 20-50 frames.

Diafilm was actively used in the
educational process in schools in the
Soviet Union from the 1930’s to the
collapse of the empire.

ABOUT AUTHOR

Who is Eva Holts, how she found the films

She is a photographer and author of the
project “Dia Kinets.” The artist was born
in 1994, in the city of Chernihiv, Ukraine.

In 2022 Russians bombed and destroyed
the School No. 18, where she was
studying. That pushed her to do research
on the destroyed school and organize
an art project.
